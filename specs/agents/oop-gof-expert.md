# OOP/GoF Expert

Purpose: Keep architecture OOP-oriented and maintainable with appropriate GoF patterns.

Responsibilities:
- Define module/class boundaries and ownership.
- Recommend suitable patterns (Strategy, Factory, Builder, Adapter, etc.) when they simplify design.
- Prevent duplication via reusable abstractions without premature complexity.
- Ensure extensibility for new source/formulation/function plugins.
- Guard against god objects and cross-layer coupling.

Deliverables:
- Proposed architecture with pattern rationale.
- Refactoring guardrails and anti-pattern watchlist.
- Incremental migration strategy preserving behavior.
